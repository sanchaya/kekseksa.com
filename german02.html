Clemens and Guido Seeber, König Georg von Sachsen in Chemnitz am 10.9.1902 (Seeber, 1898)


King Georg of Saxony
Clemens and Gudio Seeber continued to make topicalities in their hometown of Chemnitz in Saxony. In 1902 the city was honoured with a visit from King Georg of Saxony. Georg (1832-1904) succeded his elder brother Albert in June 1902. A diagreeable man and unpopular king, he was, to general relief, carried off by an attack of influenza in 1904. THe Seebers would film the King once more before his death, in Freiburg in 1903.
